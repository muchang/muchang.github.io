Chengyu Zhang received both Ph.D. and B.S. degrees from East China Normal University (ECNU) with honours (2012-2021), supervised by Prof. Geguang Pu. He was the co-founder of Shanghai Neoclub Information Technology Co., Ltd. (2015-2016) and a visiting Ph.D. student at ETH Zurich (2018-2019). He will join Advanced Software Technologies (AST) Lab at ETH Zurich in January 2022 as a postdoctoral researcher mentored by Prof. Zhendong Su.

His research interests are in:

  • Software Testing
  • Symbolic Execution
  • Formal Methods
