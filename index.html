Chengyu Zhang is a third-year Ph.D. student in East China Normal University (ECNU), Shanghai, China (2016-now), and supervised by Prof. Geguang Pu. He received his B.S. degree from East China Normal University with Excellent Graduate Award (2012-2016). He was the co-founder of Shanghai Neoclub Information Technology Co., Ltd. (2015-2016). Now, he is a visiting Ph.D. student in ETH Zurich and advised by Prof. Zhendong Su.

His research interests are in:

  • Software Testing
  • Symbolic Execution
  • Program Analysis
